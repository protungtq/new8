  Joanne Missingham was Born on May 26, 1994.  is an Australian-born Taiwanese professional Go player who has participated in international and domestic Go tournaments. In 2010 she came second in the Qionglongshan Bingsheng Cup and won the 1st Taiwan Wei-ch'i Association Women's Professionals Representative Right Ranking tournament.
