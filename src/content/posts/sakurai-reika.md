Reika Sakurai is a Japanese idol singer, actress and a former member of the Japanese idol girl group Nogizaka46. She was also the captain of the group before her departure.  Born: May 16, 1994 (age 25 years), Kanagawa Prefecture, Japan Height: 1.56 m Music group: Nogizaka46 (2011 – 2019) Nicknames: Reirei, Reika, Rekatan
